Connection status¶

In the status bar, the rotating cube gives an indication on the quality of your connection to the IPFS peer-to-peer network.

The more peers you’re wired to, the faster the cube will rotate.

Passing the mouse over the cube will show you live graphs on peers and bandwidth usage.
